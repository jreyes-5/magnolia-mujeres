Who We Are

A family of women, making things by hand

Magnolia Mujeres started in 2020 — a mother and daughter, both artists, craftswomen, designers. What began as something made for ourselves became something people kept asking about. So we made it real.

Every piece comes from that same place: hands that know what they're doing, eyes that care how it looks, and two women who make things together. We're based in Los Angeles. We love honest materials and pieces that mean something to the person wearing them.
